TIMELINE

Nov 2017

Frederick High School Maryland Science Olympiad is founded with the intent of participating in competitions and enriching STEM education within the FHS community. Dr. Cousins becomes the club sponsor.


Jun 2018

Founding members write and win a BNBI grant of $3500 to fund the kickstart of the club. The money is invested into hosting STEM days, buying construction tools, purchasing event materials, and paying for registration fees.


Oct 2018

The first ever FHS MSO team is established for the 2018-2019 competition season. New members include Jack, Ayodae, Vrinda, Nitish, and Natalie. The team wins multiple podiums in the regional competions.


Dec 2018

FHS MSO hosts the first STEM day open to the public. Students are introduced to Science Olympiad and guided through the engineering process while building catapults.


Mission

Frederick High School Maryland Science Olympiad. Founded in 2018, the club was established with two goals in mind: to create a platform for high school students to compete in Science Olympiad competitions and to foster a larger STEM community within Frederick High School.

Maryland Science Olympiad is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing students with fun and challenging STEM experiences that go beyond what is taught in the classroom. Students prepare for and compete in a wide variety of events, ranging from life science to technology and engineering. Half of the events focus on a single test topic ( ie: herpetology or meteorology) while the other half focus on hands-on build events (ie: bottle rockets or helicopters). Tournament levels include invitational, regional, state and national levels. Overall, MSO is a very worthwhile experience that can be an eye-opener for students interested in the fields of STEM.

Mission

Frederick High School Maryland Science Olympiad. Founded in 2018, the club was established with two goals in mind: to create a platform for high school students to compete in Science Olympiad competitions and to foster a larger STEM community within Frederick High School.
